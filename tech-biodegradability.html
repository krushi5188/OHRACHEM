ENVIRONMENTAL & COMPLIANCE

Environmental Profile: 100% Biodegradability

The Carbon Cycle

In modern land restoration, the goal is not just to stabilize the soil, but to restore the ecosystem. Synthetic binders that leave permanent residues act as contaminants. OHRA-BOND™ GREEN is a pure polysaccharide hydrocolloid. Chemically, it is a long chain of sugars (Carbon, Hydrogen, Oxygen).

Once applied to the soil, it acts as a food source for soil microorganisms. Over a period of 6 to 12 months, bacteria and fungi break down the polymer chains, converting them back into basic organic matter, water, and CO2. This is the natural Carbon Cycle in action.

Zero Residue

Unlike plastic netting or synthetic copolymers which break down into microplastics that persist for centuries, our vegetable binder disappears completely. It performs its function—holding the soil until vegetation is established—and then gracefully exits the environment. This "Zero Residue" profile is critical for projects in National Parks, wildlife sanctuaries, and pristine wilderness areas.
