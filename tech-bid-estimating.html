Estimating Guide: Calculating Margins for Bids

The Material vs. Labor Equation

Winning a bid is easy; making money on it is hard. Many estimators calculate their material costs accurately but underestimate their "Tank Cycles." If you assume 45 minutes per tank but the crew takes 60, your labor margin evaporates.

The Efficiency Multiplier

Using a high-viscosity, fast-loading binder like OHRA-BOND™ GREEN improves your cycle time.
Standard Method: 8 tanks/day @ 3,000 sq ft coverage each = 24,000 sq ft.
OHRA Method: 9 tanks/day (faster loading) @ 3,500 sq ft (better lubricity) = 31,500 sq ft.
Result: That is a 30% increase in daily production. When bidding, you can either pocket that 30% as pure profit or lower your bid slightly to win the job, knowing your crew will outpace the competition.
