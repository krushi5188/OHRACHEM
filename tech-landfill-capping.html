Landfill Capping: Alternative Daily Cover (ADC)

The Value of Airspace

In the waste management business, your primary asset is "Airspace"—the volume of waste you can legally pack into your permitted cell. Traditionally, regulations required covering the waste daily with 6 inches of soil to prevent odors, vectors (rats/birds), and blowing litter. However, adding 6 inches of dirt every day consumes massive amounts of valuable airspace over the life of the cell. That dirt generates zero revenue.

The Spray-On Solution

Alternative Daily Cover (ADC) allows operators to replace that 6 inches of dirt with a 1/4 inch coating of a hydro-mulch slurry. By using OHRA-BOND™ GREEN as the binder for this ADC slurry, you create a tough, durable crust that satisfies regulatory requirements for vector and odor control.

The ROI: This spray-on crust consumes virtually zero airspace. It saves the cost of excavating and hauling daily cover soil. And unlike plastic tarps (which require labor to deploy and retrieve daily), the ADC is simply sprayed on and left to decompose. It is the most efficient way to maximize the lifespan and revenue of a modern landfill.
