Field Study: 6-Month Effectiveness

The Hand-Off

Erosion control products are temporary. Their job is to hold the soil only until the permanent vegetation takes over. We call this "The Hand-Off." If the binder degrades too fast (before grass grows), you get erosion. If it lasts too long (plastic netting), it pollutes.

The Timeline

We tracked a 10-acre pipeline restoration project in Pennsylvania over 6 months.
Month 1: 100% Surface Coverage. Mulch matrix intact. No rilling.
Month 3: 80% Vegetation Density. The hydrocolloid binder was still tacky in the soil pores, holding moisture for the young roots.
Month 6: 100% Vegetation Density. The binder had visibly disappeared, fully biodegraded by soil microbes. The root systems of the established turf were now doing the work of stabilization.

Perfect Timing

OHRA-BOND™ GREEN is engineered for a functional longevity of 6 to 12 months. This matches the biological timeline of most reclamation seed mixes. It stays strong when needed, and leaves gracefully when the job is done.
